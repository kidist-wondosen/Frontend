Ethiopia

Ethiopia is a country in the Horn of Africa with a rich and diverse culture and a long history dating back to ancient civilizations. It is also the home to many UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Nature enthusiasts will love it for its beautiful landscapes, ranging from the highlands of the Ethiopian plateau to the low-lying Danakil Depression. Ethiopia is also a fantastic destination for travelers interested in cultural immersion, as this place is home to several indigenous ethnic groups with unique traditions and customs.
